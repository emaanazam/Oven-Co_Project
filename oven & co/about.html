— OUR JOURNEY —

How It All Began

From a small home kitchen to your favorite neighborhood bakery

Founded in a small home kitchen, Oven & Co. started with a simple mission: to create the most delicious baked goods using traditional methods and the finest ingredients. Maria Rodriguez, our founder and head baker, began by selling her sourdough bread at local farmers' markets.

What began as a weekend hobby quickly grew into a neighborhood favorite. The demand for Maria's artisanal breads and pastries became so great that in 2012, we opened our first bakery location in the heart of the city.

Today, we continue to honor those humble beginnings with every loaf we bake. Our commitment to quality and craftsmanship has remained unchanged, even as we've grown to serve thousands of happy customers across the region.
